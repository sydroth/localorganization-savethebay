Take Action

Fellowship

Gain hands-on experience behind-the-scenes with our Fellowship Program. Students, recent grads or anyone looking to do more for the Bay will work one-on-one with staff mentors and teams to help Save The Bay with its vital initiatives.

Individuals

Our shoreline volunteers learn to collect seeds and grow wetland plants in our native plant nurseries, plant the seedlings at key sites along the Bay, and remove weeds and trash that threaten wildlife.

Corporate

Join the more than 300 businesses and community groups that have volunteered along the shoreline with Save The Bay to improve habitat, give back to the community, and build employee teams.

You can protect and restore the Bay! To restore the shoreline, Save The Bay engages community volunteers, schools, and businesses in planting and scientific monitoring.
